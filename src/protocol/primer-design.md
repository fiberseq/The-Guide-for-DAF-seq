# Primer Design

*This section is under development. Guidelines for designing primers for targeted DAF-seq experiments will be added soon.*

Planned content includes:

- Recommended amplicon size ranges for targeted DAF-seq (typically 2-7 kb)
- Primer design considerations for regions with high cytosine content
- Strategies for multiplexed amplicon panels
- Compatibility with the [DAF-seq Protocol](protocol.md) DNA extraction step

In the meantime, refer to the methods section and supplementary materials of the [DAF-seq publication](https://doi.org/10.1038/s41587-025-02914-3) for primer design details used in the original study. You can also find primer sequences used in the manuscript in the [DAF-seq Manuscript repository](https://github.com/StergachisLab/DAF-seq-Manuscript).
